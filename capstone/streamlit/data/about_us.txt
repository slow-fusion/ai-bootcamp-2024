## Project Scope

Domain area: Understanding AI Bootcamp 2024

1. Course Structure Explainer

    - This use case explains the structure of the AI bootcamps. Users can ask and get answers on how long the duration will be, what is the passing critieria for the course, so that they may make a decision on whether to commit to the course or not.

2. Capstone Project Explainer 

    - This use case explains about the capstone project.

## Data Sources

The following will be the data sources for the use cases.

1. Course Structure Explainer

    - "Info Deck on AI Bootcamp" from the Canvas Learning Management System. As the info deck is a PDF made from Powerpoint, AISAY (a Govtech product) will be used to scrap information out from the PDF to become structured data to give to the LLM as context information.

    - "Info Deck on Project Phase" from the Canvas Learning Management System. As the info deck is a PDF made from Powerpoint, AISAY (a Govtech product) will be used to scrap information out from the PDF to become structured data to give to the LLM as context information.
